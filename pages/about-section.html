About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate (Australian Commonwealth Government RTP Scholar) in Astrophysics and Astronomy at Macquarie University, Australia, working with Prof. Daniel Zucker and Assoc/Prof. Gayandhi De Silva. My research focuses on applying machine-learning techniques and data-driven algorithms to Galactic Archaeology, leveraging data from large spectroscopic surveys like GALAH, Gaia-RVS and Gaia-ESO. Currently, I am also an Early-Career Researcher at the European Southern Observatory (ESO) in Garching, Germany, where I am collaborating with Dr. Francesca Primas on my PhD research. I actively contribute to the scientific community, serving as the Student Representative on the Astronomical Society of Australia (ASA) Council for 2023–26 and as the President of the Macquarie University Physics & Astronomy Society (MUPAS) for 2025–26.

        <article id="research-content" class="card markdown-body research-article-card"><h3>Galactic Archaeology</h3>
<p>Galactic archaeology studies the formation history of galaxies by analyzing the chemical and dynamical properties of stars.</p>
<h3>Key Questions</h3>
<ul>
<li>How did the Milky Way form?</li>
<li>What are the origins of stellar populations?</li>
<li>Can we trace ancient merger events?</li>
</ul>
<h3>Methods</h3>
<ul>
<li>Stellar spectroscopy</li>
<li>Chemical abundance analysis</li>
<li>Stellar kinematics</li>
<li>Large survey data analysis</li>
</ul>
<h3>Tools</h3>

</ul>

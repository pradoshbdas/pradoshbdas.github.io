## Chemical Tagging

Chemical tagging is a technique used to identify groups of stars that formed together in the same molecular cloud by comparing their chemical abundance patterns.

### Key Idea
Stars born in the same stellar nursery share nearly identical chemical compositions.

### Scientific Importance
Chemical tagging helps astronomers:
- Identify disrupted star clusters
- Reconstruct the Milky Way's formation history
- Understand galactic chemical evolution

### Data Requirements
- High-resolution spectroscopy
- Precise abundance measurements

### Future Work
Combining chemical tagging with Gaia kinematic data to trace stellar migration in the Galactic disk.

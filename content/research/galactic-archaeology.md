### Galactic Archaeology

Galactic archaeology studies the formation history of galaxies by analyzing the chemical and dynamical properties of stars.

### Key Questions

- How did the Milky Way form?
- What are the origins of stellar populations?
- Can we trace ancient merger events?

### Methods

- Stellar spectroscopy
- Chemical abundance analysis
- Stellar kinematics
- Large survey data analysis

### Tools

- Python
- Astropy
- Machine learning models

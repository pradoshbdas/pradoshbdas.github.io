## Accretion Structures

Accretion processes occur when matter falls onto compact astrophysical objects such as black holes, neutron stars, or young stars.

### Research Questions
- How do accretion disks transport angular momentum?
- What mechanisms trigger accretion outbursts?
- How does magnetic activity affect disk evolution?

### Physical Processes
- Magnetohydrodynamics
- Disk turbulence
- Radiative transfer

### Observational Signatures
Accretion structures can be studied through:
- Spectral line profiles
- Variability studies
- Multi-wavelength observations

### Future Work
Developing models that connect theoretical simulations with observational data from modern telescopes.

## Machine Learning for Stellar Spectroscopy

Machine learning techniques are increasingly used to analyze large spectroscopic datasets and extract stellar parameters efficiently.

### Research Goals
- Predict stellar temperature, gravity, and metallicity
- Identify unusual stellar spectra
- Automate spectral classification

### Techniques Used
- Neural networks
- Random forests
- Dimensionality reduction (PCA, t-SNE)

### Applications
Machine learning models can process millions of stellar spectra from modern surveys and identify patterns that may be difficult to detect with traditional methods.

### Future Work
Developing interpretable machine learning models that link spectral features to physical stellar properties.
